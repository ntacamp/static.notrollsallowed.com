What is No Trolls Allowed?

Have you ever been in a place where you can build an actual rocket, practice your cheese making skills and do a yoga class all at once? Well, NTA camp is the place.

No Trolls Allowed is an annual open-air hackercamp where hobbies and arts meet technology and awesomeness. It does not matter if you are programmer, traveler, chef, musician or astronaut - we will find a spot for you. In our own eyes, everyone is a hacker.

The camp is family friendly so bring everybody ;)
