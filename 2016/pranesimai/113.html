Creative Writing and Spoken Word Workshop Scena: Dirbtuvės / Workshops

The workshop can be held in both Lithuanian and English, depending on the requirements of the participants. It's a space to disclose those thoughts that might not have a place elsewhere in life. It will consist of a brief intro into the art of storytelling/spoken word poetry. Those who wish will then be able to share their own writing (stories, poetry attempts, random thoughts, scribbles etc.) which the participants should bring along to the workshop. In the second part of the workshop, we will proceed to write some new stuff using simple creative writing techniques. No experience in writing or performing is required. The aim of the workshop is to release the creative energy flow through the use of writing and reading. Bring a pen and a notepad! For an example of spoken word poetry, check out this Ted Talk: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O6jrLgvCUNs

Milda Sokolovaitė
